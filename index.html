What is SJI's Got Talent?

Do you have a special talent? SJI's Got Talent is a way for our fellow Josephians to showcase their hidden abilities to the rest of the school community.

Why should I participate?

You probably have a hidden talent that you want others to know about, but don’t have the opportunity to perform in front of their peers.

SJI’s Got Talent is here to give you that opportunity you have always wanted.

About the organisers

We are a group of IP Music students. We are organising this event and will be judging your submissions. We can’t wait for you to show us what you’ve got!
